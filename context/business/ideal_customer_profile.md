# Ideal Customer: The Career-Changer Developer

**Demographics**

-   Male, 28–45
-   White-collar job (finance, healthcare, sales, etc.)
-   Often married with kids, time-constrained
-   Based in the U.S.

**Psychographics**

-   Feels stuck or capped in current career
-   Intellectually curious, but intimidated by coding
-   Has tried Codecademy, Scrimba, or YouTube tutorials but stalled
-   Prefers structure, accountability, and mentorship
-   Wants to build a stable, remote-friendly tech career within 12–18 months

**Behavioral**

-   Listens to podcasts and learns online
-   Spends time on LinkedIn, YouTube, Reddit
-   Likely follows creators like Danny Thompson, Josh Comeau, or Theo Browne
-   Values credibility and transparency over hype

**Primary Pain Points**

-   Overwhelmed by conflicting online info
-   Doesn't know where to start or how to stay consistent
-   Skeptical of "get-rich" bootcamps but still wants a roadmap

**Motivation**

-   Wants a reliable path to a stable income and more control over life
-   Craves proof of progress (portfolio, project milestones, community)
