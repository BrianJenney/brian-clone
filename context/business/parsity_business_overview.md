# Parsity Overview

**Business Name:** Parsity.io
**Location:** Accredited in Indiana, owner based in California
**Platform:** Kajabi
**Delivery:** 100% online, quarterly cohorts (max 12 students per cohort)
**Primary Offering:** Full-stack coding bootcamp covering MERN + Testing + AI (RAG, Pinecone, Agents)
**Price:** $9,200

## Core Programs
1. **Parsity Bootcamp** – Full stack (Next.js, React, Node, MongoDB, PostgreSQL, Prisma, Testing, AI integration).
2. **Dev30 (dev30.xyz)** – 30-day JavaScript fundamentals program ($49 tripwire). Designed to convert students into the full-time bootcamp.

## Tech Stack (Educational + Internal)
- **Teaching Stack:** JavaScript, TypeScript, React, Next.js, Node, Express, MongoDB, PostgreSQL, Prisma
- **AI Modules:** Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG), Agents, Pinecone, Qdrant, Next.js AI SDK
- **Internal Tools:** Kajabi (marketing + LMS), Notion, Typeform, Google Suite, Vercel

## Key Challenges
- Low enrollment in the full program
- Reliance on organic marketing (LinkedIn, YouTube, Newsletter)
- Need for better top-of-funnel engagement and nurturing between Dev30 and Bootcamp

## Key Strengths
- Highly personalized mentorship (small cohorts, direct access to instructors)
- Focus on both **coding and career transition** (for working adults)
- Authentic founder story and strong brand trust

## Revenue Model
- Bootcamp: $9,200 tuition
- Dev30: $49 (tripwire → upsell)
- Quarterly cohorts (4 per year, up to 48 students annually)
